Our Mission

At Reading is Magic, our mission is to promote positive reading and creative writing experiences for children and adults through vibrant community events and innovative, educational programs to help inspire, encourage, and motivate families and educators to plug into the power of reading and creative writing.

Vice President -
Michael Melasi

Michael Melasi (former Marine) is a dedicated husband and father of two elementary-aged children. As the dynamic President of the Dad's Club at his children's local school, he believes engaged fathers play a crucial role in education. Michael has created a community where dads feel empowered to be actively involved, organizing events like campus cleanups, golf tournaments, and local restaurant meetings to foster connections between fathers, teachers, and the school community.

With a wealth of experience in nonprofit management and a passion for literacy, Michael's leadership and strategic vision have been pivotal in advancing Reading is Magic's mission. His extensive network and experience in Hillsborough County have the potential to expand Reading is Magic's influence and impact in Miami-Dade County. His commitment to enriching children's educational experiences is genuinely inspiring, ensuring every child can discover the joy of reading. Michael earned his Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from The University of Phoenix.

President -
Beverly Melasi-Haag

Our President and CEO Beverly Melasi-Haag truly believes that "Reading is Magic!" With over 30 years of experience as a dedicated community leader and educator, Beverly brings a wealth of knowledge and passion to our organization and a working knowledge of nonprofit management.

As a published children's book author and former President of the South Florida Writers Association, she has been instrumental in fostering a love for reading and creative writing throughout the South Florida region. Beverly is also a creative writing instructor who has developed numerous educational programs to empower families, parents, and children to discover the joy and importance of reading.

Her passion for literature and commitment to community engagement drive our vision of making reading accessible and enjoyable for everyone. Beverly earned her Bachelor's degree in English/Writing from the Union Institute University in Cincinnati, Ohio.

Empowering Parents: Guiding Your Child’s Reading Adventure

Tips for Parents: How to Foster a Love of Reading

• Create a daily reading routine: Dedicate 15-30 minutes to reading together.
‍
• Be a role model: Let your child see you enjoying books.

• Make reading fun: Use voices, act out scenes, or choose books that match their interests.

• Set reading goals: Encourage your child to track the number of books or chapters they read.

• Ask questions: Discuss the stories, ask about their favorite parts, or what they would change in the story.
